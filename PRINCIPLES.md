# Principles

A short, honest contract with the people who use torus. Every feature has to justify itself against this list. Any change that violates it should be reconsidered.

## Who this is for

torus is for producers, musicians, and anyone who wants their audio to look as good as it sounds. We're building something good and keeping it good — not chasing scale, engagement, or a funding round.

## We will never

- **Show ads** — no display ads, sponsored placements, or paid "featured" anything.
- **Sell or rent your data** — ever.
- **Run an engagement-bait feed** — no algorithmic timeline, no infinite-scroll dark patterns.
- **Load third-party trackers by default** — no surveillance analytics.
- **Use dark patterns** — sign-up, billing, and account deletion are honest and reversible.
- **Gate the core behind a subscription** — the visualizer and tools stay free.

## We will always

- **Keep the core free** — the visualizer, Conductor, and Transcriber are free to use.
- **Keep paid features cosmetic or quality-of-life** — never the essentials.
- **Charge once, plainly** — the only paid thing is the optional, one-time [Production License](./apps/visualizer/src/app/license/page.tsx) for high-quality, commercial-ready exports.
- **Process audio locally** — your audio is analyzed and rendered in your browser; we don't upload or store it.
- **Let you leave** — delete your account and data anytime, no hoops.
- **Treat accessibility bugs like real bugs.**
- **Be honest about uptime** — best-effort service, no false promises.

These principles outlive any single feature. They're a commitment, not a marketing line.
